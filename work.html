Work

This artwork portrays the idea of emotional vulnerability through two pale hands exchanging a bleeding heart. The dark background and red accents highlight themes of pain, sacrifice, and raw emotion, while the newspaper above contrasts private feelings with the external world.

This collage is inspired by Vivienne Westwood’s bold and theatrical fashion aesthetic. By layering runway imagery, striking silhouettes, and iconic accessories, the piece highlights her mix of historical references and punk-inspired glamour. The overlapping visuals create a dynamic composition that celebrates Westwood’s influence on experimental style and self-expression.

This sketch overlays a dragon-inspired design onto handwritten notes, blending everyday academic work with imaginative illustration. The fluid lines and detailed scales create a sense of movement, turning a simple notebook page into an expressive, fantasy-driven artwork.

This piece uses bold, concentric red and yellow lines to create a vibrant, almost hypnotic pattern centered around a pair of cherries. The strong geometric shapes contrast with the softer sketch on the opposite page, highlighting the tension between structure and fluidity.
